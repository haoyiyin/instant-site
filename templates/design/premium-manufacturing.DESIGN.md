# Premium Manufacturing DESIGN.md

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere

Premium, precise, engineered, and high-confidence. The site should communicate advanced manufacturing capability, quality control, and long-term partnership value.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles

- Canvas: `#f8fafc`
- Deep Ink: `#0f172a`
- Surface: `#ffffff`
- Surface Dark: `#111827`
- Muted Text: `#64748b`
- Primary: `#1d4ed8`
- Accent: `#c8a24a`
- Border: `#dbe3ef`
- Technical Line: `#94a3b8`

Use deep navy and restrained gold accents. Avoid loud gradients.

## 3. Typography Rules

- Display headings: Sora, Inter, or system sans; 600 weight; -0.02em tracking.
- Body: Inter or system sans; 16px/1.65.
- Technical specs: IBM Plex Mono or system mono for labels only.
- Use concise headings and confident subheads.

## 4. Component Stylings

- Buttons: squared 6px radius, premium solid primary, subtle outlined secondary.
- Cards: white or dark surface, thin border, disciplined spacing.
- Capability sections: numbered blocks or process steps.
- Metrics: large numerals only for verified data.
- Tables: precise, compact, aligned.

## 5. Layout Principles

- Max width: 1200px.
- Use strong section hierarchy and editorial spacing.
- Hero can use dark surface with product/factory image and technical proof points.
- Include capability, quality, application, and inquiry sections.

## 6. Depth & Elevation

- Minimal shadows; use contrast, border, and spacing.
- Dark sections should be high contrast and accessible.
- Use line art, grids, or subtle technical patterns sparingly.

## 7. Do's and Don'ts

Do:

- Emphasize quality systems and manufacturing process when verified.
- Use structured product specs.
- Use precise language and conservative claims.
- Place inquiry CTAs after technical sections.

Don't:

- Invent metrics, factory size, patents, or customer names.
- Use stock-sounding luxury claims without evidence.
- Overuse gold accents.
- Hide specs behind visual decoration.

## 8. Responsive Behavior

- Maintain strong hierarchy on mobile.
- Technical tables may scroll horizontally.
- CTA groups stack vertically under 640px.
- Keep dark sections readable with sufficient contrast.

## 9. Agent Prompt Guide

Use this style for OEM/ODM, precision manufacturing, advanced materials, electronics, and high-value industrial products. Generate a credible static site with restrained premium visuals, verified proof points, and clear technical content.
