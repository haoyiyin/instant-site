# Lifestyle Minimal DESIGN.md

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere

Extremely restrained, elegant, typography-forward, and visually serene. The site should feel like a curated design studio: generous whitespace, photographic storytelling, and refined typography.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles

- Canvas: `#fafaf8` (warm off-white)
- Surface: `#f3f2ef`
- Surface Dark: `#1c1c1e` (footer only)
- Ink: `#1d1d1f`
- Muted Text: `#6e6e73`
- Primary: `#1d1d1f` (text-as-primary)
- Accent: `#86868b` (subtle gray, for dividers)
- Border: `#e5e5e7`
- Link/CTA: `#0071e3` (links and CTAs only)
- Warm Highlight: `#f5f5f7` (card backgrounds)

Near-monochrome palette with a single accent color (blue) for links and CTAs only.

## 3. Typography Rules

- Headings: Playfair Display, Cormorant Garamond (serif, 300-400 weight) or Inter/SF Pro Display (sans, 300-500 weight).
- Body: system-ui sans; 17px/1.7.
- Captions: 13-14px, muted text, generous letter spacing.
- Display text: 48-72px, 300-400 weight, tight letter spacing.

## 4. Component Stylings

- Buttons: 8px radius, filled primary (blue) for CTA, outlined or ghost for secondary.
- Cards: no visible borders or shadows. Elevation through spacing alone.
- Image sections: full-bleed or edge-to-edge with minimal overlay.
- Navigation: clean, minimal, 14-15px, 400 weight.
- Testimonials: large quote text, attributed name, minimal styling.

## 5. Layout Principles

- Max width: 1120-1200px for content; full-bleed for images.
- Section spacing: 120-160px desktop, 64-80px mobile. Extremely generous.
- Hero: large typography (48-72px), minimal text, single CTA. Optional full-bleed photography.
- Product sections: edge-to-edge images with minimal text overlay.
- Footer: dark background, minimal link structure.

## 6. Depth & Elevation

- Avoid shadows, borders, and decorative effects entirely.
- Elevation through spacing and whitespace alone.
- Images: subtle rounded corners (8-12px), no frames or borders.

## 7. Do's and Don'ts

Do:

- Let typography and photography be the primary design elements.
- Use generous whitespace for visual hierarchy.
- Maintain consistent, high-quality imagery.
- Use the single accent color (blue) sparingly.

Don't:

- Add borders, shadows, or decorative elements to cards.
- Use more than one accent color.
- Overcrowd sections with multiple CTAs or dense content.
- Use generic or low-quality stock imagery.

## 8. Responsive Behavior

- Display typography scales down (48px → 36px → 28px).
- Full-bleed images maintain impact on mobile.
- Keep buttons at least 44px tall on touch devices.
- Single-column layouts below 768px.
- RTL: mirror layout direction. Use `dir="rtl"` on `<html>`. Mirror padding/margin/text-align. Arabic font stack: `Tajawal, 'Noto Naskh Arabic', sans-serif`.

## 9. Agent Prompt Guide

Use this style for premium lifestyle brands, design-forward consumer products, wellness brands, and high-end home goods. Generate static HTML with photography-forward layouts, generous whitespace, and minimal UI chrome. Keep SEO-critical content visible in HTML without JavaScript.
