# B2B Industrial DESIGN.md

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere

Precise, stable, trustworthy, and export-ready. The site should feel like a capable manufacturing partner: clear technical information, strong proof points, practical layouts, and direct inquiry paths.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles

- Canvas: `#ffffff`
- Surface: `#f6f8fb`
- Surface Strong: `#e9eef5`
- Ink: `#111827`
- Muted Text: `#5b6472`
- Primary: `#0f4c81`
- Primary Hover: `#0b3a63`
- Accent: `#f59e0b`
- Border: `#d8e0ea`
- Success/Trust: `#15803d`

Use blue for trust and navigation, amber only for CTAs or key highlights.

## 3. Typography Rules

- Headings: Inter, Arial, sans-serif; 600-700 weight; tight but readable.
- Body: Inter, Arial, sans-serif; 16px/1.65.
- Technical labels: 13-14px uppercase or small caps with letter spacing.
- Tables: 14-15px, clear row borders, no dense clutter.

## 4. Component Stylings

- Buttons: 6-8px radius, solid primary for main CTA, outlined secondary for downloads or product details.
- Cards: white background, 1px border, 12px radius, subtle shadow only on hover.
- Product cards: image, category label, 2-line summary, feature bullets, detail link.
- Spec tables: high contrast header row, alternating row backgrounds.
- Forms: simple fields, visible labels, clear submit CTA.

## 5. Layout Principles

- Max width: 1180-1240px.
- Hero: two-column layout with headline, proof points, CTA, and product/factory visual.
- Sections: 72-96px vertical spacing on desktop, 48-64px on mobile.
- Use grids for product categories and capabilities.
- Keep key inquiry CTA visible at the end of major sections.

## 6. Depth & Elevation

- Use borders more than shadows.
- Shadows should be subtle: `0 8px 24px rgba(15, 23, 42, 0.08)`.
- Avoid glossy effects, neon gradients, and decorative noise.

## 7. Do's and Don'ts

Do:

- Lead with product value and buyer outcomes.
- Show certifications only when provided.
- Use clear product specs and application sections.
- Include quote CTAs near product sections.

Don't:

- Invent factory scale, certifications, customers, or export countries.
- Use vague slogans without technical support.
- Hide contact options behind complex forms.
- Use overly playful visuals.

## 8. Responsive Behavior

- Collapse hero and grids to one column below 768px.
- Keep buttons at least 44px tall on touch devices.
- Tables may scroll horizontally, but core specs should remain readable.
- Navigation should prioritize Products, About, Blog, Contact.

## 9. Agent Prompt Guide

Use this style for industrial B2B export sites. Generate static HTML with clear sections, product specs, proof points, buyer-focused copy, and strong request-a-quote CTAs. Keep SEO-critical content visible in HTML without JavaScript.
