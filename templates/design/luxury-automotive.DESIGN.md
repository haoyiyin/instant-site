# Luxury Automotive DESIGN.md

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere

Dramatic, cinematic, high-contrast, and exclusive. The site should feel like a luxury showcase: bold metallic accents, immersive photography, and precision typography.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles

- Canvas: `#0a0a0a` (near-black)
- Surface: `#141414`
- Surface Elevated: `#1c1c1c`
- Ink: `#f5f5f5` (near-white text)
- Muted Text: `#a0a0a0`
- Primary: `#c8a24a` (metallic gold)
- Accent Silver: `#b8b8b8`
- Accent Bronze: `#cd7f32`
- Border: `#2a2a2a`
- Highlight: `#f8f0e0` (warm light, for CTAs on dark)

Use metallic gold for CTAs and key highlights. Reserve silver and bronze for secondary accents.

## 3. Typography Rules

- Headings: Playfair Display, Cormorant, or serif; 300-600 weight; tight letter spacing.
- Body: Inter, system sans; 15-16px/1.65.
- Spec/data labels: 12-14px uppercase, wide letter spacing (0.08-0.12em).
- Display text: 48-80px, 300-400 weight, dramatic scale.

## 4. Component Stylings

- Buttons: 4-6px radius, metallic gold primary, outlined silver secondary. Ghost buttons with subtle border glow on hover.
- Cards: dark surface (#141414), 1px border (#2a2a2a), 6px radius. No shadows—use subtle border glow on hover.
- Spec displays: structured data tables with muted labels, bright values.
- Image galleries: full-bleed, minimal overlay, cinematic aspect ratios.
- Navigation: transparent overlay on hero, solid dark on scroll.

## 5. Layout Principles

- Max width: 1400-1600px for immersive sections; 1200px for content.
- Hero: full-bleed cinematic image, large typography overlay, single CTA.
- Spec sections: side-by-side comparison or structured data display.
- Gallery: edge-to-edge, minimal spacing between images.
- Footer: dark surface, structured links, metallic accent for key elements.
- Section spacing: 96-120px desktop, 64-80px mobile.

## 6. Depth & Elevation

- Avoid traditional shadows entirely.
- Use subtle border glow: `0 0 20px rgba(200, 162, 74, 0.1)` on hover.
- Layer dark surfaces (#0a0a0a, #141414, #1c1c1c) for depth.
- Hairline borders (1px, #2a2a2a) for subtle separation.

## 7. Do's and Don'ts

Do:

- Lead with cinematic imagery and bold typography.
- Use metallic accents sparingly for maximum impact.
- Display specs and performance data with precision.
- Maintain dramatic contrast between dark canvas and bright text.

Don't:

- Use traditional shadows or flat design elements.
- Add playful or casual design elements.
- Overload sections with dense text or multiple CTAs.
- Use bright or saturated colors beyond the metallic palette.

## 8. Responsive Behavior

- Display typography scales down (80px → 48px → 36px).
- Full-bleed images maintain cinematic impact on mobile.
- Keep buttons at least 44px tall on touch devices.
- Single-column layouts below 768px.
- Spec tables scroll horizontally on mobile.
- RTL: mirror layout direction. Use `dir="rtl"` on `<html>`. Mirror padding/margin/text-align. Arabic font stack: `Tajawal, 'Noto Naskh Arabic', sans-serif`.

## 9. Agent Prompt Guide

Use this style for luxury automotive, high-end consumer goods, premium accessories, and exclusive brand showcases. Generate static HTML with cinematic imagery, metallic accent systems, and precision typography. Keep SEO-critical content visible in HTML without JavaScript.
