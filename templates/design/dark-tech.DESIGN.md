# Dark Tech DESIGN.md

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere

Dark, technical, cinematic, and future-facing. The site should feel premium and innovative while keeping product value and SEO-critical content readable.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles

- Canvas: `#070a12`
- Surface: `#111827`
- Surface Soft: `#182033`
- Ink: `#f8fafc`
- Muted Text: `#9ca3af`
- Primary: `#6366f1`
- Accent Cyan: `#22d3ee`
- Accent Violet: `#a855f7`
- Border: `#293244`

Use gradients as background accents only, not for body text.

## 3. Typography Rules

- Display: Inter, Sora, or system sans; 300-600 weight; tight tracking.
- Body: Inter or system sans; 15-16px/1.65.
- Technical labels: mono or uppercase small labels.
- Maintain high contrast for accessibility.

## 4. Component Stylings

- Buttons: pill or 10px radius, primary gradient or solid indigo, secondary glass-like border.
- Cards: dark surface, 1px border, subtle glow on hover.
- Code/tech blocks: mono font, dark inset surface.
- Stats: use only verified metrics.

## 5. Layout Principles

- Max width: 1200px.
- Hero can use gradient mesh or abstract product visual.
- Use alternating dark surfaces to separate sections.
- Keep product explanation clear and structured.

## 6. Depth & Elevation

- Use glow sparingly: `0 0 40px rgba(99, 102, 241, 0.18)`.
- Avoid low-contrast gray text.
- Keep borders visible enough on dark backgrounds.

## 7. Do's and Don'ts

Do:

- Use concise technical value propositions.
- Make CTAs visible.
- Keep product specs and FAQs readable.
- Use semantic HTML despite visual style.

Don't:

- Hide content in animations.
- Use illegible gradients for text.
- Overload with decorative effects.
- Invent integrations, benchmarks, or technical claims.

## 8. Responsive Behavior

- Collapse grids to one column below 768px.
- Reduce background effects on mobile.
- Keep nav and CTA contrast high.
- Avoid scroll-jacking.

## 9. Agent Prompt Guide

Use this style for high-tech products, electronics, AI, and advanced industrial technology. Generate static, accessible, SEO-friendly pages with premium dark visuals and clear product explanation.
