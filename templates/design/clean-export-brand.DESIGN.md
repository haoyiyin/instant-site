# Clean Export Brand DESIGN.md

## Template Fit

**Best for:** Consumer goods, homeware, packaging, light industry, export-focused B2C/B2B.

**Avoid for:** Heavy industrial machinery, fintech, dark tech, luxury automotive.

**Default dials:**
- DESIGN_VARIANCE: 5-6
- MOTION_INTENSITY: 3-4
- VISUAL_DENSITY: 4-5

**Asset strategy:** Clean product photography, lifestyle imagery, consistent aspect ratios.

**Layout families:** Two-column sections, product grids, simple testimonials, FAQ.

**Anti-slop risks:**
- Avoid aggressive sales copy or fake reviews.
- No invented MOQ, certifications, or buyer testimonials.
- Keep palette restrained; avoid mixing many colors.

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere

Clean, bright, approachable, and globally credible. The site should feel easy to browse for international buyers while still presenting products with commercial clarity.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles

- Canvas: `#ffffff`
- Soft Background: `#f8fafc`
- Warm Surface: `#fff7ed`
- Ink: `#1f2937`
- Muted Text: `#64748b`
- Primary: `#2563eb`
- Accent: `#f97316`
- Soft Accent: `#fed7aa`
- Border: `#e2e8f0`

Use a mostly white interface with warm accent areas for product highlights and CTAs.

## 3. Typography Rules

- Headings: Manrope, Inter, or system sans; 600-700 weight.
- Body: system-ui sans; 16px/1.7.
- Product labels: 13px, medium weight, muted color.
- Avoid condensed or overly technical fonts.

## 4. Component Stylings

- Buttons: rounded 10px, primary filled, secondary light background.
- Cards: soft border, 16px radius, clean image area, concise copy.
- Product galleries: consistent aspect ratios and descriptive captions.
- Testimonials/proof: simple quote blocks only when real content is provided.
- Contact panels: friendly but direct, with email and WhatsApp visible.

## 5. Layout Principles

- Max width: 1120-1200px.
- Use generous whitespace and simple two-column sections.
- Product grids should feel browsable, not catalog-heavy.
- Include visual rhythm: hero, product categories, advantages, process, FAQ, CTA.

## 6. Depth & Elevation

- Use soft surfaces and light borders.
- Shadows: `0 10px 30px rgba(15, 23, 42, 0.06)` for feature cards only.
- Avoid dark heavy blocks unless used as a footer.

## 7. Do's and Don'ts

Do:

- Use simple buyer-friendly language.
- Highlight product range, customization, packaging, MOQ, or shipping only when known.
- Make contact options easy to find.
- Keep product imagery consistent.

Don't:

- Overload pages with technical jargon.
- Invent buyer reviews, certifications, or MOQ details.
- Use aggressive sales copy.
- Mix too many colors.

## 8. Responsive Behavior

- Product grids become one or two columns on mobile.
- Keep CTA buttons full-width on small screens when useful.
- Keep nav compact and simple.
- Avoid horizontal scrolling except for data tables.
- RTL: mirror layout direction. Use `dir="rtl"` on `<html>`. Mirror padding/margin/text-align. Arabic font stack: `Tajawal, 'Noto Naskh Arabic', sans-serif`.

## 9. Agent Prompt Guide

Use this style for consumer goods, homeware, packaging, and light industry export sites. Prioritize clean product presentation, buyer trust, simple navigation, and static SEO-friendly HTML.
